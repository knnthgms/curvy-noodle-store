I've been using stickers since I can remember. I was probably 8 when I had my first sticker sheet and began collecting them.

Now, as a 30+ adult woman, I still find them a source of extreme happiness and use them to fulfill my adult planning needs. This means using these stickers to depict what's going on in my life, important events, fun memories, and just adding a bit of cheer to my spreads.

<!-- TODO: Add image: (decorative photo supporting the blog post) -->

Creating stickers has been a wonderful and creative experience. While I have so much more to learn, I would like you all to be a part of my journey from the beginning and experience the same joys that I do with using stickers.

I hope you or your loved ones find them useful and have an absolute blast using them.
